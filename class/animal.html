DOG

Dogs are domesticated mammals and are often considered
"man's best friend" due to their long history of companionship with humans.
They belong to the Canidae family and are descendants of wolves,
although over thousands of years of breeding, they have evolved into various
breeds with different sizes, shapes, and temperaments.

CAT

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that have been domesticated by humans for thousands of years.
Known for their agility, independence, and playful nature, are one of the most popular pets worldwide.

BIRDS

Pet birds are popular companions for their vibrant colors, intelligence,
and unique ability to bond with humans. They come in a wide variety of species,
each with different care needs, personalities, and abilities.
